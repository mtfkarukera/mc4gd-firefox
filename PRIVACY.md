# Privacy Policy — Magic Clipper for Google Drive

**Last updated:** 2026-05-17

## No data collection
Magic Clipper for Google Drive does not collect, store, or transmit
any personal data to external servers.

## Google Drive access
The extension requests access to your Google Drive solely to upload
files you explicitly select from your browser. No file is read,
modified, or deleted without your direct action.

## OAuth token
The OAuth token is stored locally in your browser via
`browser.storage.local` and never leaves your device.

## Open source
This extension is fully open source. You can audit the code at:
https://github.com/mtfkarukera/magic-clipper-drive

## Contact
Open an issue on GitHub for any privacy-related question.
