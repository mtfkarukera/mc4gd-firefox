Privacy Policy – Magic Clipper for Google Drive

Last updated: June 2026

1. No Data Collection

Magic Clipper for Google Drive does not collect, track, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing activity to external or third-party servers. All processing and request execution occur locally within your browser.

2. Google Drive Access & Scope Justification

The extension requests OAuth 2.0 access to your Google Drive via the full https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive scope. This permission is used solely to find or create the folder named "Imports Magic Clipper" and upload your selected files to it.

Why is the full scope needed? The full drive scope is required to search for pre-existing folders created during previous sessions or on other devices. The narrower drive.file scope is insufficient because it only permits the extension to view files/folders that it created during the current session, which would result in creating duplicate import folders. We do not read, edit, or delete any of your other Google Drive files.

3. Local Storage

Your OAuth2 access token, expiration timestamps, cached Google Drive folder ID, and manual language preferences are stored locally on your device via browser.storage.local. This data never leaves your device except when authenticating directly with Google APIs.

4. Cookies and Tracking

This extension does not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking tools.

5. Contact & Open Source

This extension is fully open source. You can audit the code and report any issues or privacy concerns on our GitHub repository:
