Privacy Policy – Magic Clipper for NotebookLM

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data collected

Magic Clipper for NotebookLM does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to third-party servers. All processing occurs locally in your browser.

2. Google Drive access

When you use the Google Drive import feature, the extension requests OAuth 2.0 access limited to the drive.file scope, which only allows access to files you explicitly select. No file content is stored outside your browser session.

3. Cookies and tracking

This extension does not use cookies or any tracking technology.

4. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, contact the developer via the project repository:
